To provide wide range of business opportunities to microentrepreneurs to create a league of professional business leaders that will empower more business leaders by producing and distributing superior quality and innovative products that are unique to the market by implementing standardized business processes that will ensure growth of the company by organizing socially responsible programs that will impact and benefit the lives of the Filipino people
